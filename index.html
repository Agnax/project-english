10 Tips

For how to use the internet and social media

1

Keep your computer safe from malicious software and do not open messages or strange files.

2

Protect your personal information. Data and images that identify you have a value that should not be given away.

3

Upload the images and write what you want on the Internet but make sure before it does not bother anyone and that you do not mind that anyone can see it.

4

Do not answer the provocations. Avoid conflicts. If something bothers you, disconnect and tell a trusted adult person.

5

Configure the privacy options of your social networks well. It's very important.

6

Do not share your keys. Keep them secret. Change them from time to time.

7

The webcam gives a lot of information about you. Measure very well with whom you use it.

8

Think that everything you see on the Net is not true. Anyone can put anything. Contrast the information.

9

Be cautious if you do not know the person on the other side. Beware of friends of friends.

10

In no case you cite yourself for the first time alone with someone you have met on the Internet.
